Cookies & Sessions

Cloudflare Services

Whorder uses local storage to remember the list ID. Whorder does not read, write, or interact with any cookies, or local sessions; however, KPNC Technology uses Cloudflare services to provide users with the best experience and to protect our own infrastructure. Cloudflare sits in between your client and our origin servers and may read, write, or interact with their own cookies, local sessions, or local storage for a variety of reasons.

For more information see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy...

Technologies

Cloudflare Workers/KV, Angular

The backend of Whorder uses both Cloudflare Workers and their Key Value database for snappy API requests and backend operations. The frontend of Whorder uses Google's Angular framework for a powerful and performant experience.
